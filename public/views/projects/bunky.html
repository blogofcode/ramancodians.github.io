About the Project

What was the Problem ?

Managing attendance in college is a B**ch!

What is the Solution?

Creating an easy to user application that analysis your attendance and bunking patterns and guide you houw to maintain your attendance and also help you to Bunk those borring classes.

Details about the project

Christ University has one of the strictest createrria for attendance. 85%!! I paid attendance fine for all semesters. This is not just my problem but every student in our college. Therefore, it needs to be solved. Bunky is a unique project for various reasons. I learned how to work with Cordova, Custom APIs and developing apps for all platforms(Web/Android/iOS).

This was the first time I explored Google's Material Design & Apple's iOS Design Specification. Hands on exprecince with Cordova/Phonegap taugh me how precious the resources are on the mobile and this is the time to follow the well know mobile first approach for the cross-platform apps as well.
